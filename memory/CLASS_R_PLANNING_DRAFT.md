## WHAT IS CLASS R?

**Class R** is a Permitted Development Right (PDR) that allows conversion of agricultural buildings to flexible commercial use (Class E) without a full planning application. It requires a "Prior Approval" application to the Local Planning Authority (LPA).

**Key benefits:**
- Faster than full planning (8-12 weeks vs 6-12 months)
- Cheaper (£500-2k vs £5k+ for full application)
- No public inquiry risk

**Limits:**
- Maximum 500 sq metres per agricultural unit
- Must have been agricultural building for 10+ years

## SUBMISSION STEPS

1. **Identify your LPA** (Local Planning Authority) - likely your district council
2. **Download application form** from their planning portal
3. **Prepare documents** (see checklist above)
4. **Submit online** via planning portal or email
5. **Pay fee** (typically £500-£1,000 for prior approval)
6. **Wait** (LPA has 8 weeks to respond)
7. **If approved** — commence use
8. **If refused** — can appeal or submit full planning application
